Wizarducks Demo Released!

A game with magic, puzzles, and ducks

A Duck can cast Spells

You are a duck, and a wizard! This game would be terrible at naming itself if the duckling couldn't use spells!

Spells can solve puzzles

The objects that your magic interacts with, can either either bar your progress or aid you on moving forward.

And you can play with the dog

We fiercely believe you should be able to play with your doggos. It took great effort to not make it the main mechanic of the game.

It would mean the world to us if you played our demo. It is very short, and even you quitting halfway would inform us a lot.

Currently we're testing mechanics and if the game is working fine on the supported platforms.

We'd also love to hear what you liked, what you hated, and you'd like to see in the future.
